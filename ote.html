OTAs, Booking Facilitators & Other Intermediaries: A Mismatch for Purposeful Travellers and Travel Enablers

OTAs, Booking Facilitators & Other Intermediaries:
The Parasitic Interference in the Traveller-Enabler Equation

Parameters of Advantage for Travel Enterprises OTAs/ Travel Aggregators Grasshopper365 & www.TripMemo.com
VISIBILITY
A product needs to be visible to reach its potential customers. Various strategies promote visibility, including advertise- ments, PR, etc. But the huge costs they come with may not make it economical for small or medium sized travel businesses Visible only in search engine sites www.TripMemo.com owns a well-advanced, community sourced Content Management System that is updated in real-time. When an entrepreneur lists a business on www. TripMemo.com using Grasshopper365, the business gets a microsite set up on www.TripMemo.com. Travellers visiting www. TripMemo.com for destination information or itineraries, etc. could view these microsites and initiate direct interactions with the hosts.
BRANDING
Branding is essential for uniqueness and to lead the competition, by imprinting unique attributes in the customer per- spective. Branding maintains customer loyalty and prompts repeat orders from buyers. OTAs/ Travel Aggregators do not promote brands. Most try to sell off commodities offered by the businesses even without proper brand tags. Thus, customer loyalty or brand identity cannot be created. Grasshopper365 is keen on highlighting, promoting and nurturing brands which lead to building customer loyalty. It thus marks Customer Relationship Management as an important module.
COMMISSION
There is a great difference between surviving in business and thriving in it. It is almost similar to ‘existing’ versus ‘living happily.’ OTAs/ Travel Aggregators consume 15-30% of booking amounts as commission. At times, inventories are offered at cheap rates. This may induce more number of room sales, driving more commis- sion and market share for OTAs/ Travel Aggregators, but damages the brand value of listed businesses. Grasshopper365 is a commission-free On-line Travel Enabler (OTE) system that lets the listing enterprises manage their inventories directly and even determine their prices. The app makes it easier for entrepreneurs to manage sales and cash flow depending on their priorities and needs.
DIRECT SALES
DIRECT SALES The best way to make good business profits is by direct selling, especially with low costs incurred on advertisements and promotions. OTAs/ Travel Aggregators do not allow direct sales by enterprises. Most demand travel companies to sign contracts that say NO to direct sales. Grasshopper365 app can carry links to web- sites and/or social media pages of its listing enterprises. Enquiries on-site, purchases, or check-ins can be handled directly from the app, promoting direct sales.
OPERATIONS
OPERATIONS The success of a business largely depends on its operations. Technology makes it easier to carry out operations of a travel business, but spending huge amounts on software or tools that integrate invento- ries and their bookings can be uneconomical. OTAs/ Travel Aggregators do not have the software or apps that offer smooth working techniques for their listing enterprises. Grasshopper365 considers operations to be the backbone of success in any business. The app has various operational features that help its listing businesses carry out actions with utmost efficiency.
COLLABORATIONS
Healthy business collaborations catalyse the growth of a business entity. It augments the referrals gained and the resultant revenue of the enterprise. There is no platform currently available in the travel industry that facilitates such collaborations which would improve the quality of the services provided by the enablers as well as the quality of the travel experience for the travellers. OTAs are neither concerned about such collaborations, nor are they about the quality of the travel experience or the specific needs of individual travellers. With Grasshopper365, all 5 pillars of travel business, viz. travellers, travel guides, accommodation services, taxi & transport services and Travel equipment rental services, are interconnected. Travel enablers can build business relations by the name of TripMemo Associates in the ecosystem and attend to every travel requirement of the travellers, even if they are beyond their serviceability, by seeking aid from their associate travel enablers.
