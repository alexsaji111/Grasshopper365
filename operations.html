ACCOMMODATION SERVICE

(APPLICABLE TO ACCOMMODATION SERVICE PROVIDERS)

Booking

It is a graphical register of all the upcoming bookings in the property. The number of rooms occupied and vacant under different room categories for specified dates will be shown along with the booking summary for the current month or week. The variable pricing for different dates could also be viewed in this section. It also allows a quick option to check-in and check-out the guests.

Check-in

This section handles all the checked-in bookings as well as upcoming bookings. It not only gives the complete details of the checked-in guests and their activities in the property but also allows users to check new guests in.

Check-out

The Check-out section handles all the check-outs from the property for the current/selected date. It helps with the bill generation and payment procedures as well.

House-keeping

This section keeps track of the housekeeping tasks in the property. The number of chores assigned to separate staff, along with their checklists, comprises this section. This is further classified into Routine and Check-out Housekeeping.

Staff

This section deals with the property’s staff management. It comprises the list of staff and their designation and other personal details. The access given to individual staff to use select modules in the registered Grasshopper account could be regulated from this segment.

Food & Bev

This section keeps the complete record of all the purchases that customers make from the restaurant in/attached to the property. It creates separate bills for the restaurant-related purchases of the customers, which could be paid separately at the restaurant or together with the balance payment at check-out.

Day Book

This section documents all the daily expense sheets as managed by the staff. This replaces the traditional practice of having to carry numerous paper-bits which are very likely to go missing. The corresponding expense bills may also be attached to each entry by the staff, thereby negating the possibility of discrepancies and missing receipts.

Reports

This section comprises all the business-related system-computed reports of the property for the selected month. It is a vital business intelligence tool that will ever be needed for business decisions.

Promotions

This section is a single point from which all the promotions of the property could be handled. It helps to manage the promotions on social media pages, business website, TripMemo business timeline, etc. It also facilitates the operation of G2 Connect, which is the API developed to integrate promotions directly with Google Hotel Ads. However, this segment is currently inactive and is under construction.

EQUIPMENT RENTALS

(APPLICABLE TO TRAVEL EQUIPMENT RENTALS)

Booking

It is a graphical register of all the upcoming bookings at the outlet. The number of products and kits booked for specified dates will be shown along with the booking summary for the current month or week.

Rent-Out

This section handles all the upcoming and current rent-outs at the outlet. It not only gives the complete details of the rentals, the corresponding customers, and their activities but also allows users to rent out products to new walk-in customers.

Drop-Ins

This section handles all the product returns for the current/selected date. It also helps with the bill generation and payment procedures.

House-keeping

This section keeps track of the housekeeping tasks in the property. The number of chores assigned to separate staff, along with their checklists, comprises this section. This is further classified into Routine and Check-out Housekeeping.

Staff

This section deals with staff management for the rental outlet. It comprises the list of staff and their designation and other personal details. The access given to individual staff to use select modules in the registered Grasshopper account could be regulated from this section.

Day Book

This segment documents all the daily expense sheets as managed by the staff. This replaces the traditional practice of having to carry numerous paper-bits which are very likely to go missing. The corresponding expense bills may also be attached to each entry by the staff, thereby negating the possibility of discrepancies and missing receipts.

Reports

This section comprises all the business-related system-computed reports of the outlet for the selected month. It is a vital business intelligence tool that will ever be needed to make viable business decisions.

Promotions

This section is a single point from which all the promotions of the rental outlet could be handled. It helps to operate the promotions on social media pages, business website, TripMemo business microsite, etc. It also facilitates the operation of G2 Connect, which is the API developed to integrate your promotions directly with Google Hotel Ads. However, this segment is currently inactive and is under construction.

TRANSPORT SERVICE

(APPLICABLE TO TOUR AND TRANSPORT SERVICE PROVIDERS)

Booking

It is a graphical register of all the upcoming tour bookings for the registered enterprise. Details of all the booked tours for specified dates will be shown along with the booking summary for the current month or week in this section.

Running

This section handles all the on-going tours from the registered enterprise. It not only gives the complete details of the tours, including the details of the customers, status/progress of the tours and tour activities but also helps to register new bookings from walk-in customers.

Completed

This is a register of all the completed tours from the registered enterprise up till the current date for the selected month. It also helps with the bill generation and payment procedures.

Staff

This section deals with staff management for the registered travel enterprise. It comprises the list of staff and their designation and other personal details. The access given to individual staff to use select modules in your Grasshopper account could be regulated from this segment. In the case of guiders, the users can add partners or assistants in this segment, and work collectively using the all-module-access option.

Day Book

This section documents all the daily expense sheets. This replaces the traditional practice of having to carry numerous paper-bits which are very likely to go missing. The corresponding expense bills may also be attached to each entry by the staff, thereby negating the possibility of discrepancies and missing receipts.

Reports

This section comprises all the business-related system-computed reports of the registered enterprise for the selected month. It is a vital business intelligence tool that will ever be needed to make business decisions.

Promotions

This section is a single point from which all the promotions of registered travel enterprises could be handled. It helps to manage the promotions on social media pages, business website, TripMemo business microsite, etc. It also facilitates the operation of G2 Connect, which is the API developed to integrate promotions directly with Google Hotel Ads. However, this segment is currently inactive and is under construction.
